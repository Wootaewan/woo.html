In a Genius interview, he stated that his artist name of Pop Smoke is a combination of Poppa, a name given to him by his Panamanian grandmother, and Smocco Guwop, a nickname from childhood friends.





[7] He began his music career in 2018 when visiting a Brooklyn recording studio with fellow rapper Jay Gwuapo. After Gwuapo got high on drugs, he fell asleep. Pop Smoke went into the booth to try rapping for the first time, just to see if he could do it. He used a beat he got from 808Melo's YouTube channel and recorded his track titled "Mpr (Panic Part 3 Remix)".[6] On January 28, 2019, he released "Flexin'".[8] Meanwhile, Pop Smoke befriended producer Rico Beats, who was acquainted with record executive Steven Victor.

The three set up an interview, and in April 2019, Pop Smoke signed to Victor Victor Worldwide, a subsidiary of Universal Music.

[9][10] On April 23, 2019, Pop Smoke released his breakout single, "Welcome to the Party", that was produced by 808Melo.[11] Two remixes of the song were later recorded, with one featuring Nicki Minaj and the other featuring Skepta.[11] Pop Smoke released his debut mixtape Meet the Woo on July 26, 2019.

[12] From October to December 2019, Pop Smoke would release a plethora of singles, including "War" with Lil Tjay,[13] and "100k on a Coupe" with Calboy.[14] On December 27, 2019, Pop Smoke appeared on Travis Scott's Cactus Jack Records compilation album JackBoys on the song "Gatti" which was also accompanied by a music video.[15][16] "Gatti" debuted and peaked at number 69 on the US Billboard Hot 100, giving Pop Smoke his first Hot 100 appearance.[17][18] On January 16, 2020, Pop Smoke released "Christopher Walking".[19] On February 7, 2020, Pop Smoke released his second mixtape Meet the Woo 2, with features from Quavo, A Boogie wit da Hoodie, Fivio Foreign and Lil Tjay.[20][21][22] The mixtape debuted at number seven on the US Billboard 200, earning Pop Smoke his first top-10 hit in the United States.[23][24] Five days after its release, a deluxe edition was released with three new songs, each featuring a guest appearance, consisting of Nav, Gunna, and PnB Rock.[25][26][27] Pop Smoke teased to social media his debut headlining concert tour Meet the Woo Tour to promote both his mixtapes.[28][29] The tour was planned to begin in the US in March, and end in the UK in April.
